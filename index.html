Frequently Asked Questions

How long are the calls?
Calls last up to 15 minutes to keep them focused and helpful.
Can I have unlimited calls?
No. We limit calls so the service stays fair and accessible for everyone.
What should I bring to the call?
A notepad, clear questions, and an open mind.
What if I cannot make the time?
Just email us and we’ll happily reschedule.
What if my course is niche?
If we don’t have someone immediately available, we’ll find a suitable match as soon as possible.
Are master's students available?
Not yet, but we will always try to match you with someone who can help.
How is the call set up?
All calls are held on Zoom or Google Meet. You'll receive a link once confirmed.
Will CV & Personal Statement reviews be done on the call?
No, students are there to guide you. If you need your CV or personal statement reviewed, contact us and we’ll assign someone to review it.
